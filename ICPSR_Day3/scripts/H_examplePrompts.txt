## Chain of Thought example and contrast
4. Question: The cafeteria had 23 apples. If they used 20 to make lunch and bought 6 more, how many apples do they have?  Please explain your logic in steps.
Contast
4a. The cafeteria had 23 apples. If they used 20 to make lunch and bought 6 more, how many apples do they have? Dont give me any information except the answer.

## Contrastive Chain of Thought example - sometimes fails!
5. Question: James writes a 3-page letter to 2 different friends twice a week.  How many pages does he write in an year?
Explanation: He write each friend 3*2=6 pages a week.  So he writes 6*2=12 pages every week.  That means he writes 12*52=624 pages a year.
Wrong Explanation: He writes each friend 12*52=624 pages a week. So he writes 3*2=6 pages every week.  That means he writes 6*2=12 pages a year.
